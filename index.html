About Us

A Legacy Worth Keeping

B&W Legacy Farms is a family-owned operation rooted in the farmland of New Haven, Indiana. Passed down through generations, we carry forward the values of hard work, integrity, and responsible land stewardship that have defined our family from the beginning.

We believe farming is more than a business — it's a commitment to the land, the community, and the future. Every acre we tend is a reflection of that belief.

What We Do

Our Operations

We operate a full-cycle grain farming operation in Allen County, Indiana — from field management to storage.

Grain Farming

We grow corn, soybeans, and wheat across our operation, applying proven agronomic practices to maximize yield while maintaining the long-term health of the soil.

Cash Rent Partnerships

We partner with landowners through cash rent agreements, offering reliable, professional stewardship of your land. If you're looking for a dependable tenant farmer, we'd love to talk.

On-Site Grain Storage

Our on-site grain storage capabilities allow us to manage harvest logistics efficiently, providing flexibility in marketing and reducing post-harvest risk.
